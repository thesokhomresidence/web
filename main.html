Why BKK1 is the Best Area to Base Yourself

BKK1 is consistently the top recommendation for expats in Phnom Penh. Here's why our location puts you at the heart of everything:

Strong Internet Connection

For your remote or online work, you'll benefit immensely from our free, high-speed WiFi, ensuring a reliable and stable connection for all your professional needs.

Job Opportunities

BKK1 is home to numerous international schools and language centers, offering a wide range of English teaching positions (full-time, part-time, flexible hours). It's also a hub for NGOs and other organizations, providing diverse work possibilities.

Networking & Community

As Phnom Penh's premier expat hub, BKK1 is the easiest place to connect with other travelers, professionals, and locals. You'll find countless cafes, co-working spaces, and social events where you can network and make friends.

Central Location

The Sokhom Residence is ideally situated, making it truly central to everything in Phnom Penh. This means easy access to transport for your country-wide adventures (like heading to the coast for Koh Rong!), as well as local attractions.

Vibrant Lifestyle

BKK1 is a foodie paradise with an incredible array of international and local restaurants, charming cafes, and lively bars. You'll also be close to:

  • Aeon Shopping Mall
  • Independence Monument
  • Royal Palace
  • Riverside
